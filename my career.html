My Dream Career

An aspiring Website Designer


The inspiration behind the reason I'm pursuing a BSIT course is that I'm aiming to become a website designer upon completing my college education. This determination developed during my senior high school year as I got exposed to gadgets because of the pandemic. At first, I wasn't really good at it. However, during this time, I eventually found joy in designing my digital activities, PowerPoint presentations, and even our group's website, thinking that I wasn't skilled enough at that time. On the other hand, my creativity improved as I gained a proper of designing using various digital tools. I enjoyed that part that eventually I'm slowly learning it and realizing that I'm already good at designing digital works and have gained new strength.

Another strength I'm capable of lies in adaptability, considering that in the field of IT, there is a constant innovation of new ideas and technologies. Nonetheless, I acknowledge certain weaknesses, particularly in terms of limited knowledge, time constraints, and a lack of experience. Being at the rapid pace of advancement in the world of technology, my journey might be difficult, particularly in learning the use of various coding languages. But with my continuous learning and involvement in different activities related to my course, I view these shortcomings as opportunities for growth, and I have a strong will to transform them into my strengths.
